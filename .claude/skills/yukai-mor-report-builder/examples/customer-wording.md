# Customer-safe wording examples

## Instead of blaming
Avoid: "The customer did not provide access."
Use: "The activity is pending required access confirmation."

Avoid: "The vendor caused the delay."
Use: "The timeline was impacted by dependency on vendor confirmation."

Avoid: "The system is unstable."
Use: "The service experienced intermittent behavior and is under continued monitoring."

## Incident update style
"The team identified the affected component, performed initial containment, and is validating the permanent fix. No data loss has been observed based on currently available evidence."
